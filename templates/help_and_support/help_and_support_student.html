Getting Started Guide

  • After registering, verify your email and log in. Complete your profile for a personalized learning experience.
  • Use the class code shared by your teacher to join the class and start learning.
  • Access your class materials from the dashboard, including assignments, notes, and recorded lectures.

Best Practices

  • Stay on top of deadlines by checking your assignments regularly and setting reminders.
  • Break your study sessions into manageable time blocks, review class materials regularly, and reach out for help when needed.
  • Use a strong password, enable two-factor authentication, and be cautious about sharing personal information online.
